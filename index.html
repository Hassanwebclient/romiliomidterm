Romilio's Family Story

More than 25+ years of experience

Let’s step back in time to 19th century Beirut, when the smell of our kitchen's cook were in the air for the first time. If you ever wondered how Italian cuisine reached Lebanon, we’re about to tell you a story about the iconic Romilio's Kitchen.

Back in 1800, an Lebanese man called Mr. Chamseddin opened the first ever restaurant in Beirut. His love for sharing new delicacies with the world won the hearts of the Lebanese people and their beloved capital.
